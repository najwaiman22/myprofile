Patience & Tradition

Dendeng taught me the virtue of patience. The first time I attempted this recipe, I rushed through the process and ended up with burnt, tough pieces. My grandmother laughed gently and said, "Good things take time, sayang." We started over together, and she showed me how to wait for each stage, how to listen to the sizzle, and how to trust the process. Now, every time I make dendeng, I remember her wisdom‚Äîthat the best things in life, whether it's food or relationships, require patience and care.
