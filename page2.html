About Me

My name is Nurul Najwa Iman binti Shudirman, but I'm more fondly known as Najwa Iman or Nawa. Born and raised in Selangor for 16 years before finding a new chapter in Kedah, I carry both places as part of my story. I was born on 28 April, on a night when the moon rested in its Waning Gibbous phase. I'm currently pursuing a Diploma in Information Management at UiTM Kedah.

I'm actively involved in organisational and event management, both on campus and beyond.

Hobby and Passion

I love spending time on things that spark joy and creativity. Some of my favorite hobbies include volunteering, cooking, and writing stories. These activities let me explore new ideas, help others, and express myself in fun and meaningful ways.

Beyond my hobbies, I'm passionate about people and making things work smoothly. I love turning ideas into action, organising events, and helping a team succeed together. Learning is also a big part of my life. Leadership, information management, communication, and self-development are all areas I enjoy diving into. I'm looking forward to diving into the world of business, but I haven't found the right circle yet. If you know someone who fits this description, let me know! I thrive on anything that lets me grow, contribute, and leave a positive impact.

My Growth Path

I'm learning to lead with clarity. I'm working on communicating better, making decisions with confidence, and balancing strength with humility. I'm growing into someone who pauses before reacting, values honesty over comfort, and knows when to be soft and when to be firm. Every experience whether in class, organisations, or daily life, is shaping me into a more disciplined, resilient, and grounded person.

Values I Live By

Honesty keeps me real. Compassion keeps me human. Discipline keeps me moving. Growth is something I chase every day. I try to respect others, maintain harmony, and take responsibility for my actions. Strength is quiet, steady, and intentional, and I build it step by step.
